# 📊 EuroMart Stores Dashboard | Google Looker Studio

## 📖 Project Overview

The **EuroMart Stores Dashboard** is an interactive Business Intelligence dashboard developed using **Google Looker Studio** to analyze sales performance across multiple European regions. The dashboard provides real-time insights into sales, profit, customer transactions, product performance, and regional trends through interactive visualizations.

The dashboard helps business users monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), identify high-performing products and regions, evaluate customer segments, and support strategic business decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze overall sales and profitability.
- Monitor key business performance indicators.
- Compare regional sales performance.
- Identify top-selling product categories.
- Track customer transactions and purchasing behavior.
- Support business decision-making through interactive visualizations.
